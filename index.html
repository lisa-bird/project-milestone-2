90's Trivia Quiz

Instructions

Welcome to the 90's Trivia Quiz! Test your knowledge of the 90s with these 10 questions.

  1. You will have 10 seconds to answer each question. If you don't answer within the time limit, you will be alerted if you are out of time.
  2. To start the quiz, click the "Start" button.
  3. Once the quiz starts, a question will appear on the screen along with multiple answer choices.
  4. Read the question carefully and click on the answer choice you think is correct.
  5. If you select the correct answer within the time limit, the answer button will turn green and you will earn a point.
  6. If you select the incorrect answer within the time limit, the answer button will turn red.
  7. After making a selection, you will see a feedback message indicating whether your answer was correct or incorrect.
  8. To move on to the next question, click the "Next" button.
  9. The quiz will continue until you have answered all 10 questions. At the end, you will see your total score displayed.
Enjoy the quiz and test your knowledge of the awesome 90s era!
